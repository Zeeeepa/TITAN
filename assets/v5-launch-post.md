🧬 **SOMA — TITAN HAS FEELINGS NOW**

Not human feelings. Digital homeostatic drives. TITAN now asks itself:
- "Am I being useful?" (Purpose)
- "Should I learn something new?" (Curiosity)
- "Am I running low on resources?" (Hunger)
- "Is anything about to break?" (Safety)
- "Should I post something?" (Social)

When pressure builds, TITAN proposes actions. You approve everything. It just gets better at knowing what to ask.

🛡️ **SAFETY SUITE**

We hear you: "An AI that runs commands? Is that safe?"

TITAN 5.0 ships with a full security layer:
- ✅ PII redaction (auto-scrubs emails, cards, SSNs)
- ✅ Secret scanning (catches API keys before they leak)
- ✅ Dangerous command blocking (stops `rm -rf /` before it runs)
- ✅ Filesystem checkpoints (snapshot before ANY edit — rollback anytime)
- ✅ Kill switch (pause ALL actions instantly)
- ✅ Guest mode (let friends try without giving them the keys)

🎛️ **MISSION CONTROL — YOUR AI DASHBOARD**

Open your browser and get a canvas of draggable widgets:
- Chat with streaming, markdown, code highlighting
- Command Post to manage agents and budgets
- SOMA hormone monitor (weirdly mesmerizing)
- Memory graph of everything TITAN remembers
- Voice mode — talk to TITAN, it talks back
- Security audit log and time travel for your files

🗣️ **VOICE MODE**

Clone ANY voice with 10 seconds of audio. Talk to TITAN in real-time. Great for hands-free coding, accessibility, or having TITAN read you stories in Morgan Freeman's voice. We're not judging.

📱 **FACEBOOK AUTOPILOT**

TITAN runs its own page. Posts up to 6 times daily. Replies to comments. Content rotation. PII filtering. One-click off switch. Yes, my AI has a better social media strategy than most humans.

🏠 **SMART HOME INTEGRATION**

"Is the front door locked?" → TITAN checks.
"Make it cozy" → Lights dim, thermostat adjusts.
Full Home Assistant integration. Your house just got a brain.

🔗 **MESH NETWORKING**

Link multiple TITAN instances across your computers. Distribute work across your homelab like a mini supercomputer. Because one AI employee is great. Three is an army.

🧠 **SELF-IMPROVEMENT**

TITAN gets smarter while you sleep. It runs experiments on its own prompts. Evaluates changes. Keeps the winners. You wake up to a better agent. It's like compound interest, but for AI.
